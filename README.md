Love-Tank-Game-
===============

Love Tank Game.  Shoot the citizens with love missiles to bring them on your side!

Keys
---
Left and Right: Moves you left and right

Space: Shoot

Shift: Speed Boost while holding left and right.

Javascript and HTML5 Game.
